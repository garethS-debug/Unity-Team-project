The trainstation, a dreamland where any sleeping human can commune with the recently departed spirits of their family to help their loved ones, and themselves, move on. 
\n
All one has to do is enter the grove behind the train station and set a campfire, their family will be called to this fire and they can then begin their journey to overcome grief.
